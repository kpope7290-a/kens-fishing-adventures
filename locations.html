Chicago fishing facts

  • Shoreline: 22 miles of Lake Michigan access and multiple public harbors.
  • River fishery: Revitalized Chicago River holds carp, catfish, bass, and some walleye.
  • Stocked lagoons: Family‑friendly lagoons across the city are regularly stocked.
  • Popular species: Coho/Chinook salmon, steelhead, brown trout, perch, bass, bluegill, crappie, carp, channel catfish.
  • Year‑round action: Open water most seasons; ice fishing possible in lagoons/sloughs during cold snaps.
